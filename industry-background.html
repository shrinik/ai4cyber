Hospital Industry

The hospital industry is a vast sector that touches many areas of healthcare. Patients are admitted for short-term stays with the goal of getting well enough to leave the facility with a cured or managed condition.

Overview

The hospital industry, also referred to as acute care, is a variety of services that treat patients for short-term conditions. The goal of acute care is to have patients leave a facility able to live their lives independent of medical assistance. It can include many different types of ailments and acute care facilities include hospitals, out-patient surgical centers, emergency medicine, trauma centers, urgent care and clinics. We have chosen to focus on Hospitals for our project.

Services Provided by the Hospital Industry

  • Emergency Medical Care
  • Maternity Care
  • Nursing
  • Surgery
  • Pharmacy
  • Laboratory, X-Ray and Diagnostic Procedures
  • Specialty Services such as cardiology wards, cancer centers, pediatrics, and rehabilitation

Size of the Industry

  • The global health industry is worth 12 trillion as of 2021 and is the fastest-growing industry.
  • The hospital services market is set to hit 16.45 trillion by 2027
