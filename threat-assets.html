Critical Asset Identification

The following IT assets have been deemed to be critical for the hospital industry

Following five critical assets were identified

# Information/Assets IT infrastructure Information Systems Patient Data IoT Medical Devices Mobile Devices
1 What is included in the asset? This includes hardware like servers, networks and software such as operating systems Software applications and data required for the administrative functioning of the hospital. This includes patients PHI, PII and other types of medical records. These are connected devices and systems used for patient monitoring and diagnostics, etc. These are used for communications, entering patient data, etc.
2 Why is it important? Hospitals with superior IT infrastructure may provide higher-quality care. The provision of access to paid medical evidence databases and accessibility to the internet were strongly associated with hospital quality of care Information systems can improve cost control, increase the timeliness and accuracy of patient care and administration information, increase service capacity, reduce personnel costs and inventory levels, and improve the quality of patient care. The importance of accurate data in healthcare can mean the difference between life and death to that specific patient and to the larger collective of patients as a whole. IoT enables healthcare professionals to be more watchful and connect with the patients proactively. Mobile devices and apps provide many benefits for HCPs, perhaps most significantly increased access to point-of-care tools
3 What are the ramifications if critical assets are breached? Hospitals will shut down. Email goes down, so different departments or campuses can’t communicate about patient tests and records; etc. Hospitals will shut down.  Email goes down, so different departments or campuses can’t communicate about patient tests and records; etc. Hospital will shut down. Patients cannot be serviced without the access and exchange of their data/ lab tests etc. Hospital will not be able to function without its equipment if equipment is required to be online.  Hospital will not be able to function without its communication devices.
4 Who is using the assets? All major technology-based assets use the IT infrastructure. Hence, users will indirectly include all hospital staff and patients.
